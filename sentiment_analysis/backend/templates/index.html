About Vibechecker

Vibechecker is a sentiment analysis tool designed to help users understand the emotional tone of their movie reviews. By analyzing the text, the tool can determine if the review is positive, negative, or neutral. This is especially useful for movie enthusiasts, critics, and content creators who want to gauge audience reactions or evaluate personal responses to films.

This project demonstrates how machine learning can be applied to real-world problems in sentiment analysis, providing valuable insights into user-generated content. Try it out by entering your review above and let Vibechecker reveal the sentiment behind your words.
